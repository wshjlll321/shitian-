# Anti-AI Rules

## Rule Intent

These rules prevent the site from looking AI-generated, generic, over-prompted, or detached from real Shitian UAV assets.

## Mandatory Rules

- Every visual and content choice must be grounded in Shitian UAV products, parameters, cases, or real scenes.
- Specificity is mandatory. Use model names, locations, tasks, payloads, times, and outcomes.
- Real media must be preferred over generated abstraction.
- Design must be sparse, deliberate, and content-led.
- The site must feel authored by a premium industrial brand, not assembled by a prompt.
- AI-generated assets, if used, must look like realistic industrial photography and must not contain incorrect aircraft structures or unreadable fake text.
- Copy must avoid generic future-tech language.
- Modules must have clear business purpose.

## Forbidden Rules

- Do not use generic slogans such as "empowering the future", "explore infinite possibilities", "redefine intelligence", "technology changes the world", or "smart future starts here".
- Do not use abstract AI aircraft that do not match actual products.
- Do not use fake labels, unreadable interface text, hallucinated mechanical details, or impossible drone structures.
- Do not create generic futuristic backgrounds.
- Do not create content that could fit any robotics, AI, SaaS, or smart manufacturing company.
- Do not use vague icons for serious operational scenarios.
- Do not use fake data charts without source.
- Do not use decorative complexity to imply sophistication.

## Required Specificity

Each major section must include at least one of:

- Product model
- Real scenario
- Specification
- Case location
- Case outcome
- Operational task
- Company proof
- Contact or conversion path

## Failure Conditions

The page fails if:

- Removing the company name makes it indistinguishable from a generic AI tech site.
- The visuals rely on imaginary technology instead of real equipment.
- The copy contains mostly abstractions.
- The same layout could sell cloud software, AI chips, electric scooters, or consulting.
- It uses style before substance.
- It has no trace of real agricultural, forestry, emergency, maritime, or aviation operations.

## Generation Constraints

- Run the AI Generated Smell Detection Checklist before finalizing any page.
- Replace vague claims with product, data, case, or scene evidence.
- Replace abstract visuals with product or operation visuals.
- Delete decorative elements that cannot justify their existence.
