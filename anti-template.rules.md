# Anti-Template Rules

## Rule Intent

These rules prevent the new website from becoming a foreign trade template, SaaS template, generic B2B site, or ordinary UAV catalog.

## Mandatory Rules

- Every page must have a reason to exist.
- Every section must have a specific content purpose.
- Every module must be shaped by Shitian UAV products, scenarios, and cases.
- Page rhythm must be varied.
- Content hierarchy must be editorial, not template-based.
- Visual composition must change according to narrative need.
- Conversion must be contextual.
- Product and scenario proof must replace generic selling points.

## Forbidden Rules

- Do not use ordinary three-column feature cards as a primary structure.
- Do not use repeated "title + subtitle + cards + button" section patterns.
- Do not use generic hero plus feature grid plus testimonials plus FAQ layout.
- Do not use generic icon feature walls.
- Do not use equal-height sections throughout.
- Do not use old foreign trade site elements such as "hot products", "recommended news", sidebar categories, or floating customer service widgets.
- Do not use decorative cards for every piece of content.
- Do not create fake testimonials.
- Do not create placeholder partner logos.
- Do not create generic "why choose us" modules unless each point is backed by evidence.
- Do not create FAQ filler without real sales questions.

## Required Differentiation

The new site must be different from a template through:

- Real product hierarchy
- Real operation cases
- Scenario-based structure
- Cinematic visual pacing
- Premium industrial typography
- Restrained motion
- Specific conversion paths
- Structured technical specifications
- HIZONE system narrative

## Failure Conditions

The site fails if:

- It could be sold as a generic website template.
- The same layout could be used for any factory or SaaS company.
- It does not make T280 / S270 memorable.
- It presents applications without cases.
- It has no editorial rhythm.
- It repeats cards until the page feels mechanical.
- It lacks large product or scenario visuals.

## Generation Constraints

- Before creating a section, write its purpose in one sentence.
- Reject any section whose purpose is only "show features".
- Replace feature cards with story, comparison, metric, scene, or case modules when possible.
- Use cards sparingly and only for repeated records.
- Audit section patterns after every page draft.
